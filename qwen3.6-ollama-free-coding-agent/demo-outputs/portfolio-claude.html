I'm a product & UI designer with eight years of experience building digital products for startups and growing teams. My work lives somewhere between Figma, after-effects curves, and design system audits at 2 AM.

Previously design lead at Folio, senior designer at Klim Studio. Now independent — taking on a handful of engagements per year where I can be end-to-end on the work.

I care about typography you can read, motion that earns its weight, and systems that don't break the moment someone ships a new feature.
